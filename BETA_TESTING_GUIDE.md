## üêõ REPORTING PROBLEMS

When you find something wrong, please tell us:

### For a BROKEN THING:
**Say**: "I clicked the 'Add to Cart' button on the Banarasi Silk product and nothing happened"

**Instead of**: "Something's broken"

---

### For CONFUSING DESIGN:
**Say**: "On mobile, the form field labels are hard to see above the input boxes"

**Instead of**: "The design is confusing"

---

### For SLOW PERFORMANCE:
**Say**: "When I opened the Shop page, it took about 5 seconds to load all the products"

**Instead of**: "It's slow"

---

### The BEST Feedback:
- What page were you on?
- What did you try to do?
- What actually happened?
- What did you expect to happen?
- What device/browser were you using?

## üì± MOBILE TESTING TIPS

If you're testing on a phone:

1. **Portrait mode**: Make sure site works in normal phone orientation
2. **Landscape mode**: Turn your phone sideways - does it still work?
3. **Small screen**: iPhone SE users, does it work on your tiny screen?
4. **Touch targets**: Can you tap buttons without hitting something else?
5. **Scrolling**: Does the page scroll smoothly?
6. **Hamburger menu**: Does it open/close smoothly?
7. **Form inputs**: Are text boxes big enough to type in?
