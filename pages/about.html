About Sanskriti.Traditional

Preserving India's Cultural Heritage, One Craft at a Time

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sanskriti.Traditional was born from a deep passion for preserving India's rich artistic heritage. We recognized that countless skilled artisans across India were struggling to find markets for their exquisite handcrafted goods.

Our platform bridges the gap between these talented craftspeople and discerning customers who appreciate authentic, handmade products. Every purchase directly supports artisan families and helps keep traditional crafts alive for future generations.

Today, we work with over 500 artisans across 15 states, offering a curated collection of authentic handicrafts that celebrate India's diverse cultural traditions.
