Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

We want you to love every piece you receive. However, we understand that sometimes things don't work out. This policy explains how returns and refunds work for our handcrafted products.

Important Note: All our products are handcrafted by skilled artisans. Minor variations in color, texture, pattern, and size are natural characteristics of handmade items and reflect their authenticity. These are not considered defects.

1. Return Window

You have 10 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

After 10 days, we cannot accept returns, but we'll still help with any issues you're facing.

2. Eligible Items for Return

You can return an item if:

  • Wrong item delivered ‚Äì We shipped something different from your order
  • Damaged during shipping ‚Äì The item arrived broken or torn
  • Manufacturing defect ‚Äì A genuine flaw that affects functionality or appearance (not natural variations)
  • Quality issue ‚Äì The product doesn't match the description or photos significantly

Items That Cannot Be Returned

  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper care
  • Items without original tags or packaging

3. Return Conditions

For a return to be accepted, the item must be:

  • Unused ‚Äì Not worn, washed, or altered
  • Original condition ‚Äì With all tags, labels, and packaging intact
  • Clean ‚Äì Free from stains, odors, or damage
  • Complete ‚Äì All accessories and documentation included

4. How to Initiate a Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out within 10 days of delivery via:

  • Email: returns@sanskrititraditional.in
  • Contact form: Contact Us
  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210

Provide your order number, photos of the item (if damaged), and reason for return.

Step 2: Approval

We'll review your request within 24-48 hours and send you return instructions.

Step 3: Ship It Back

  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  • Use the return shipping label we provide (if eligible for free return shipping)
  • Drop it off at the courier location

Step 4: Inspection & Refund

Once we receive and inspect the item (typically 3-5 business days), we'll process your refund.

5. Refund Process

Refund Timeline

  • Inspection: 3-5 business days after we receive the item
  • Refund initiation: Within 2 business days of approval
  • Refund in your account: 5-7 business days (depends on your bank)

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card ‚Üí Back to the same card
  • UPI/Wallet ‚Üí Back to the same account
  • Cash on Delivery ‚Üí Bank transfer (we'll collect your account details)

What's Refunded

  • Product cost ‚Äì Full amount
  • Original shipping ‚Äì Only if the return is due to our error (wrong/damaged item)
  • Return shipping ‚Äì Covered by us if the return is due to our error
Return Shipping Costs: If the return is due to a change of mind or sizing issue, return shipping charges may apply and will be deducted from your refund.

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you'd like a different size, color, or product:

  1. Return the original item
  2. Place a new order for the item you want

This ensures you receive your new item faster without waiting for the return process to complete.

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  • Take photos immediately (packaging + product)
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • We'll arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or full refund

No questions asked. We stand behind our quality.

8. Lost or Delayed Shipments

If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery time:

  • Check your tracking status on our Track Order page
  • Contact us if it's been more than 2 days past the expected delivery date
  • We'll work with the courier to locate your package or issue a refund/replacement

10. Final Sale Items

Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for return or refund, except in cases of damage during shipping or manufacturing defects.
